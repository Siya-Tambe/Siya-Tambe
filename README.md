## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🎯 [PriceSense-AI](https://github.com/Siya-Tambe/Pricesense-AI)
> *Know when to buy. Let AI decide.*

AI price tracker for 8 Indian platforms — Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, Croma and more. Paste a product URL, and it builds price history automatically, forecasts the next 7 days, and gives you a **Buy / Wait / Watching** verdict with confidence levels.

`Python` `FastAPI` `SQLite` `BeautifulSoup` `Chart.js` `Tailwind CSS` `JavaScript`
